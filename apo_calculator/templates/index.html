The Capsules Module

The capsules module allows the production of tailor-made capsules for your customers. Finished medicinal products (tablets) can be added to the FMP database and they can be chosen as ingredient for your capsules. A wizard will guide you through all important steps and no manual calculations have to be performed anymore. The volumentric measuring cylinder method will be used for this type of production.

The Suppositories Module

The suppositories module allows the production of individualized suppos. Substances from the substance database that have a displacement factor can be chosen as ingredient the suppos. A wizard will guide you through all important steps and no manual calculations have to be performed anymore. The displacement factor method will be used for this type of production.

The Uniformity of Mass Module

The uniformity of mass of single-dosed drug forms is performed by weighing 20 capsules or suppositories individually and determining the mean weight with the corresponding thresholds. The Apo-Calculator allows adding such a calculation to a production using an input form. All calculations are performed automatically and the results are shown in a visualization graph.

Learn more


The Mass Balance module

During the production of capsules or suppositories, some of the powder mix or suppository mass may get lost during transfers or by sticking to surfaces. Using the Mass Balance Module, you can identify such a loss by comparing the results of the manual production steps with the uniformity of mass.

Medicinal Products Licencing Ordinance Value

The MPLO value is a risk-based approach based on Swiss law to determine the risk associated with an extemporaneous preparation. Five factors must be set, and they are finally multiplied to generate the MPLO value, which should be under 100 for a cantonal manufacturing license. Manual calculation becomes obsolete using this module.

The ALT-Calculator

The prices of extemporaneous preparations are regulated by Swiss law. The "Arzneimittelliste mit Tarif" defines the rules for the calculation of the price. The lengthy process is automatized using the integrated price calculation.

The Substance Database

The integrated substance database contains all substances listed in the "Arzneimittelliste mit Tarif" including their prices and relevant physicochemical properties such as the displacement factor or freezing point depression, if applicable.

The Finished Medicinal Products (FMP) database
