The Uniformity of Mass Module



The uniformity of mass of single-dosed drug forms is performed by weighing 20 capsules or suppositories individually and determining the mean weight with the corresponding thresholds. The Apo-Calculator allows adding such a calculation to a production using an input form. In the below example, the input data for suppositories (left two columns) is used to determine the relevant calculated data (right two columns). An algorithm determines whether the requirements from the Pharmacopeia are met, and the test result is displayed on the bottom right. The uniformity of mass module furthermore shows the distribution of weights in a custom-made graph with green (i.e., up to +/- 1 threshold), orange (i.e., from +/- 1 up to +/- 2 thresholds), and red (over +/- 2 thresholds). The three highest and lowest values are displayed as blue dots.
