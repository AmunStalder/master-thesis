Uniformity of Mass

According to Chapter XX of the European Pharmacopoeia, the uniformity of mass must be given for medicines that have been produced in small scale. The specifications depend on how much the contents of one capsule weighs. For capsules that weigh below 300 mg, only 2 out of 20 randomly chosen capsules may differ by more than 10%, but none may differ by more than 20% of the average content.
