The Mass Balance Module for Capsules



During the production of capsules, some of the powder mix may get lost during transfers or by sticking to surfaces. The uniformity of mass only considers the distribution of masses, meaning that it does not consider a possible loss of bulk. This means that the uniformity of mass test can be passed even if, e.g., 20 % of the bulk was lost during a transfer.
For that, the mass balance test is performed to identify a possible loss of bulk. In the case of capsules, the mean content of a capsule is multiplied by the total amount of produced capsules. This value is then compared to the total mass of produced bulk, and the difference should not be more than +/- 10 %.
